**Where is it?** - Nestled in the Florida Panhandle along the Emerald Coast, Panama City sits at a geographical sweet spot offering a blend of crystal-blue waters and lush, green landscapes. Look towards the northwest of Florida to spot this gem【6】.

---

**Brief History** - Born from the merging tales of Spanish explorers and coastal economies, Panama City's rich history is deeply intertwined with the sea. It’s a place where the past and present sail together, evident in its museums and historic trails that trace back to its days as the town of Harrison【13】.
